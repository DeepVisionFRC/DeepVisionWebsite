What is DeepVision?

DeepVision (Also known as DV8 or Team 7308) is a community based high school robotics team located in Silicon Valley. With over 15 students from 5 schools, DeepVision provides hands-on experience for budding engineers through the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). In FRC, teams are given a unique challenge each year that is played in a sports-like game, and are given 6 weeks to design, build, and program a working industrial grade robot to compete with. 2019 will be DeepVision's second season competing in FRC. You can learn more about FRC here.
